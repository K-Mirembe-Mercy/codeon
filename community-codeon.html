    Turn Your Skills into Income

    CODEON is not just a learning platform — it opens real doors to income for talented students across Africa. Here are the ways you can earn.

    👑

    CODEON Pro Subscription

    Platform revenue: Students subscribe for UGX 10,000–25,000/month for mentorship sessions, certificate issuance, and advanced challenges. Instructors earn a commission per student they mentor.

    PRO TIER
    🎓

    Become a CODEON Mentor

    Top-ranked students (Level 10+) can apply to become paid peer mentors. Host 1-on-1 tutoring sessions for beginners and earn UGX 5,000–15,000 per session through the platform.

    MENTOR PROGRAM
    🏢

    Corporate Hackathon Sponsorship

    CODEON sells hackathon naming rights and prizes to Ugandan tech companies (MTN, Airtel, Stanbic, Andela). Students compete, companies discover talent. Win for everyone.

    CORPORATE
    📜

    Verified Certificate Sales

    Certificates cost UGX 15,000–30,000 to issue with verification. Employers pay to verify certificates in bulk. Students who refer employers earn a 10% affiliate commission.

    CERTIFICATES
    🔗

    Affiliate & Referral Program

    Earn UGX 2,000 for every paying student you refer. Top affiliates get featured on the homepage and earn monthly passive income. Students with 50+ referrals earn a Partner badge.

    AFFILIATE
    🛍️

    CODEON Merch & Job Board

    Top students can buy CODEON-branded merchandise to show their rank. A job board (coming soon) lets companies pay to post opportunities exclusively to verified CODEON graduates.
